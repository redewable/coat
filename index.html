The COAT Process™

From walkthrough to reveal

Every project follows our proven five-step system.

01

Walkthrough

We meet you on site, face-to-face. We inspect the surface, take measurements, and help you choose the right system based on traffic, moisture, design goals, and timeline.

02

COATPrep™

Proper prep is everything. We grind, repair cracks, and moisture-test every slab. No shortcuts — just the foundation for a floor that lasts.

03

Install

Our crews work with precision and respect. Clean job sites, clear communication, and craftsmanship you can see in every corner.

04

Final Reveal

We walk the finished floor together. Every edge inspected, every detail confirmed. You don't pay until you're thrilled.

05

COATCare™

We stay with you after install. Our maintenance guidance and support ensure your floor looks stunning for years to come.
