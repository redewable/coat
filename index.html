The COAT Process™

From walkthrough to reveal

Every project follows our proven five-step system, ensuring excellence every time.

01

Walkthrough

On-site meeting, surface inspection, measurements, system recommendation based on traffic, moisture, design goals, timeline.

02

COATPrep™

Grinding, crack repair, moisture testing — no shortcuts.

03

Install

Precision craftsmanship, clean job sites, clear communication.

04

Final Reveal

Walk the floor together, inspect every edge — you don't pay until you're thrilled.

05

COATCare™

Post-install maintenance guidance and ongoing support.

Most residential projects cure in one day, with full use in 24-48 hours. Commercial projects vary based on size and phasing.
