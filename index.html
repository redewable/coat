The Process

From first call to final walkthrough, here's how we deliver.

01

Consultation

We visit your site, assess concrete condition, discuss your needs, and recommend the right system. Free, no-obligation.

02

Preparation

Industrial diamond grinding, crack repair, moisture testing, and full surface profiling. This is where 80% of quality lives.

03

Application

Multi-layer system: primer, 100% solids epoxy base, broadcast media, and UV-stable polyaspartic topcoat. No shortcuts.

04

Walkthrough

Final inspection together, detailed aftercare instructions, and warranty documentation. We don't leave until you're satisfied.

Built on principle, not just concrete.

COAT was born from a simple belief: the floor beneath your feet should be built with the same integrity as the life you're building on top of it.

After nearly a decade in the flooring industry—covering everything from hardwood to tile to carpet—we saw an opportunity. The garage, the workshop, the warehouse floor. The spaces everyone else overlooked. We chose to master the one floor nobody wanted to do, and do it with excellence.

Today, COAT is a family business serving Texas homeowners, builders, and commercial property owners who refuse to settle. We're Aggies, we're Kingdom-minded, and we believe that how you build matters as much as what you build.
